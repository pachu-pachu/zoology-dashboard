Kerala University B.Sc. Zoology S2

This schedule organizes the entire syllabus of Animal Diversity II (Chordates) - Course Code: ZO1241 along with Complementary Botany II and Complementary Chemistry II. Target each day logically.

Day 1: Protochordata \& Cyclostomata

Core Zoology
  • Protochordata Affinities: Hemichordata, Urochordata, Cephalochordata classification keys.
  • Balanoglossus: External features, ciliary feeding structure, anatomical profiles.
  • Type Study - Ascidia: Morphology, physiology, and highly-weighted Retrogressive Metamorphosis essay.
  • Agnatha Details: Salient features of Cyclostomes, comparing *Petromyzon* and *Myxine*, and the Ammocoetes larva.

Day 2: Pisces \& Amphibia

Core Zoology
  • Type Study - Scoliodon (Shark): External morphology, respiratory system, afferent/efferent arterial branches, and brain anatomy.
  • Piscian Adaptations: Accessory respiratory organs in fishes, migration behaviors (Anadromous/Catadromous), and Dipnoi evolutionary links.
  • Class Amphibia: Salient structural classifications, parental care (behavioral & physical) and Neoteny theories.

Day 3: Reptilia, Aves \& Mammalia

Core Zoology
  • Class Reptilia: Identification formulas for poisonous vs non-poisonous snakes of Kerala (*Naja naja*, *Vipera*), venom setups, and biting dynamics.
  • Class Aves: Morphological, anatomical, and metabolic flight adaptations, double respiration, and flight physics.
  • Class Mammalia: Characteristics of Prototheria, Metatheria, and Eutheria. Mammalian dentition patterns and specialized aquatic modifications.

Day 4: Plant Anatomy \& Physiology

Botany Complementary
  • Meristems: Structural properties, and apical organization hypotheses (Histogen, Tunica-Corpus theories).
  • Anomalous Growth Structures: Anatomical features of *Bignonia* (furrowed xylem) and *Boerhaavia* stems showing anomaly.
  • Water Mechanics: Stomatal action mechanisms (active $K^+$ transport theory) and Cohesion-Tension systems.

Day 5: Coordination Chemistry \& Reactivity

Chemistry Complementary
  • Coordination Theory: Werner's core theory, IUPAC complex naming rules, and VBT parameters.
  • Electronic Shifts: Inductive, electromeric, mesomeric, and hyperconjugation displacements.
  • Organic Substitutions: Nucleophilic $S_N1$ and $S_N2$ reaction dynamics, and Benzene electrophilic nitration.

Day 6: Common English \& Second Languages

Languages
  • Prose & Poetry: Standard analysis of the English textbook prose essays and poetry modules.
  • Academic Communications: Business/academic letters, CV structural designs, and structural grammar repairs.

Day 7: KU Exam Questions & Practical Diagrams

Final Drill
  • Diagram Checklist: Speed practice of *Scoliodon* brain, *Bignonia* and *Boerhaavia* cross-sections.
  • KU PYQ Run: Solve previous 3 years' short answer questions within a strict 60-minute trial constraint.
